Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photography and videography services

How far in advance should I book your services?

For wedding photography and videography, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance to secure your date, especially during peak wedding season. For other services, 2-4 weeks notice is typically sufficient, but availability may vary.

What is your payment policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking date, with the remaining balance due one week before the event. For portrait sessions and smaller projects, full payment is due at the time of booking.

How long does it take to receive the final photos/videos?

For weddings and large events, delivery time is typically 4-6 weeks. Portrait sessions and smaller projects are usually delivered within 2 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

Yes, we are available for travel both domestically and internationally. Travel fees may apply depending on the location. Please contact us for a custom quote for your destination photography or videography needs.

What happens if there's bad weather on the day of our outdoor shoot?

We monitor weather conditions closely before all outdoor shoots. If severe weather is forecasted, we'll work with you to reschedule at no additional cost. For weddings, we always have backup plans for indoor locations.

Do you provide raw, unedited files?

We do not provide raw, unedited files as part of our standard packages. Our editing process is an essential part of our artistic vision and final product. However, we ensure you receive a comprehensive selection of high-quality edited images.
