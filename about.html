Our Story

SHUBH CLICKS Photography & Videography was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate visual storytellers with a shared vision: to capture life's most precious moments with creativity, authenticity, and technical excellence.

What began as a small team specializing in wedding photography has grown into a full-service visual studio offering a wide range of photography and videography services. Our journey has been defined by our commitment to continuous learning, embracing new technologies, and most importantly, connecting with our clients to understand their unique stories.

Today, we're proud to have documented thousands of special moments for clients across the country, from intimate weddings to large corporate events, always with the same dedication to quality and personal service that has defined us from the beginning.
